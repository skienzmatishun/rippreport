Dear City of Fairhope Single Tax Colony and members:

Dear Sirs,

I graduate from your last class on Fairhope Single Tax Colony taught by Mr. Dean Mosher. I found it interesting; however the hypocrisy of local government versus Fairhope Single Tax Colony always seems to be an issue. I wanted to join immediately after the course. However, I read that Mr. Lee Turner became the President of FSTC.

I was shocked at how Mr. Turner could sit on Planning and Zoning and also Fairhope Single Tax simultaneously. I remind you that Council Person Burrell recently was trying to acquire $1 million of FSTC membership dues for the so-called "park" known as the Dyas Triangle.

The Planning Commission is broken. We have for years suffered lawsuit after lawsuit and wasted millions of dollars on their poor decisions and their inability to enforce their own rules. The Mayor of Fairhope is currently being sued by personally by AMIC, our former insurer for misrepresentation, among other things. Mr. Turner is putting himself squarely in the position where he could as well find himself a defendant.

The Lee Turner Planning Commission agrees 99 percent of the time with whatever Mayor Kant approves. The FSTC now is going to hold a kangaroo court and try two of its members as non-worthy. They broke the cardinal rule: Never ask questions.

The entire incident surrounding the two members could be resolved simply by providing the information and FSTC files to the public. The Planning Commission offers the citizens of Fairhope three minutes per year (you heard that right) to speak to non-agenda items.

This resolution was passed in order to silence my criticisms and comments. Now I see the same Lee Turner at FSTC and the first thing they want to do is throw out the members who are inquiring about the truth.

I was not asked by anyone to become involved in this issue. However, the same bad players in the City have now infiltrated your FSTC. Beware—it's their pocket book they're looking at.

Paul Ripp
