This week for our 100th Episode we have a special guest: Alabama State Auditor Jim Zeigler(R). He is poised to announce that he will challenge Alabama Governor Kay Ivey for the Republican nomination (we talk prisons, masks, personal freedoms, and the Constitution (The Good One and the Alabama One).

42 Million SARS-COV-2 infected in the USA with 672,970 deaths. The Delta variant has Gulf Coastal Hospitals out of ICU beds. Routine surgeries and procedures are being delayed to the detriment of the 55% of Alabamians who make up our workforce.

The FDA’s advisory panel hosted an 8 hr Skype meeting and suggested that we not all run for a booster shot and took, for the first time, a cautious approach to these MRNA Vaccines, not to be confused with a “Regular Vaccine”.

The City of Fairhope entered into a consent agreement with ADEM related to the operation of their waste water treatment collection system and treatment plant. Seeing as the effluent outfall is within eyesight of the Public Beach. And some Norm McDonald.
